Frequently Asked Questions

What is Altrexis?

Altrexis is a technology and consulting company that helps businesses grow through innovative software, data, and digital solutions.

What does Altrexis offer?

We build custom software, digital products, and brands. Our services include cloud infrastructure, data analytics, product design, branding, and information security.

Which industries does Altrexis work with?

We work with clients across various industries including technology, healthcare, finance, manufacturing, real-estate, e-commerce, and more. Our team has the expertise to adapt our services to meet the unique needs of different sectors.

What kinds of businesses does Altrexis support?

Altrexis works with businesses of all sizes, especially small and medium enterprises (SMEs), helping them scale with smart, secure, and future-ready technology.

How do you ensure the security of our data?

Data security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure access controls, and regular security audits. Our team follows strict data protection protocols to ensure your information remains confidential and secure.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is tailored to each project's specific requirements. We offer flexible engagement models including fixed-price projects, time and materials, and dedicated team options. Contact us to discuss your project and receive a customized quote.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-launch support and maintenance services to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. Our support packages include regular updates, bug fixes, and technical assistance as needed.
